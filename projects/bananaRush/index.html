Banana Rush

Welcome to our Game!

Welcome to our ICS3U Summative website. This website will contain information about our programming process along with our game, Banana Rush. Banana Rush is a game in which you play as a monkey that must dodge obstacles and collect bananas. The goal is to advance to the next level by dodging the obstacles. The concept of Banana Rush takes heavy inspiration from Geometry Dash but has our own twist to it. Good Luck and have fun!
